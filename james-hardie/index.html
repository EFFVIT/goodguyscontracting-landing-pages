Why Homeowners Choose James Hardie Siding

James Hardie fiber cement siding is the #1 brand of siding in America—engineered specifically to stand up to harsh coastal and seasonal conditions like what Long Island homeowners face every year.

Exceptional Durability

Fiber cement outperforms traditional vinyl and wood siding—it won't warp, crack, or succumb to impact damage over time.

Moisture, Pest & Rot Resistant

Unlike wood, James Hardie siding is engineered to resist the moisture, salt air, and insects that Long Island homes face year-round.

Long-Lasting Factory Color

ColorPlus Technology bakes the finish onto every plank—color that resists fading, chalking, and peeling for decades without repainting.

High-End Curb Appeal

Available in a wide range of styles and colors that replicate the look of wood, stucco, or masonry—without the maintenance headaches.

Adds Real Home Value

Quality fiber cement siding consistently ranks among the highest-ROI home improvements—buyers notice and appraisers respond.

Low Maintenance

Compared to wood siding that requires regular painting and upkeep, Hardie siding dramatically reduces ongoing maintenance costs.
