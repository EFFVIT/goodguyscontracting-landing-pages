What Affects Siding Cost

Several factors influence the total cost of a siding replacement project on Long Island.

Home Size

Larger homes require more materials and labor.

Material Choice

Fiber cement siding generally costs more than vinyl but offers greater durability.

Trim & Architectural Details

Board and batten or decorative trim increases project complexity.

Existing Siding Removal

Older siding removal or structural repairs can add cost.
